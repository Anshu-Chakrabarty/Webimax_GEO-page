Frequently Asked Questions

What is Generative AI SEO?
Generative AI SEO (also called Generative Engine Optimization or GEO) focuses on optimizing your content so AI platforms like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini can understand, reference, and recommend your brand when generating answers to user questions.
How is GEO different from SEO?
Traditional SEO focuses on ranking web pages in search results. GEO focuses on structuring content, authority signals, and entity data so AI systems can cite your brand directly when generating answers.
Can AI search drive website traffic?
Yes. When AI systems reference your brand as a trusted source, users often click through to learn more. AI visibility can increase credibility, brand awareness, and qualified organic traffic.
Is AI search replacing Google?
AI search is not replacing Google but evolving it. Modern search experiences now combine traditional search results with AI-generated answers, which is why businesses need to optimize for both SEO and AI search visibility.
