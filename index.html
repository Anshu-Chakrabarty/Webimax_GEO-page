Expert Insights on GEO and SEO

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the process of optimizing content so that AI-powered platforms such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini can identify, summarize, and cite your website as a trusted source of information.

How is GEO different from SEO?

While SEO focuses on ranking in traditional search engines like Google, GEO focuses on ensuring your content is structured in a way that AI systems can interpret and recommend within conversational responses.

Why do businesses need GEO and SEO together?

Search behavior is evolving. Businesses must optimize both for search engine rankings and for AI-generated recommendations to maintain visibility across modern discovery platforms.
